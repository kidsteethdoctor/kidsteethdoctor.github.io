Community Outreach

Classrooms

We understand that oral health is an inseparable part of a child’s overall health and welfare. Thus, our preschool prevention program has been teaching children about good oral habits for nearly 40 years. As a gift to the community, Dr. Ching continues the Happy Smiles Program for preschools, elementary schools, parent groups, and career days.

During school hours, we can send our staff members to your school. A special interactive presentation will be made by our Tooth Fairy and her helper, Doug the Dog. They will motivate children to brush, floss, and make healthy food choices. We will make sure your students will go home with a “Super Brushers” certificate and a positive experience!



Parent Groups

After hours, Dr. Nicholas Ching is available to visit parent groups in order to give advice about good oral hygiene and nutrition for children and to review the latest research and technology related to pediatric dentistry. He also enjoys speaking to future professionals at career day, giving informative presentations and answering any questions you may have.

If you would like us to visit your school, group, or special event, then please call our school coordinator at (510) 524-4633 or email kidsteethdoctor@gmail.com to set up a visit for your group. We are located at 7001 Stockton Avenue, Suite 3 in El Cerrito, and our office hours are at the top of our home page. We look forward to hearing from you!
